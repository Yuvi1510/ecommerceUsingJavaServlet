Frequently asked questions

How can I track my order?

Sign in and open My Orders. Select an order to see status and tracking. You will also get email updates when your order ships.

What is your return policy?

Unopened items in original packaging can be returned within 7 days of delivery. Some categories may have different rules; check the product page before you buy.

How do I cancel an order?

Go to My Orders and choose Cancel if the order is not yet shipped. After dispatch, you can refuse delivery or request a return once it arrives.

Do you deliver across Nepal?

We deliver to major cities and many towns. Enter your address at checkout to see options and estimated delivery time.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major cards, mobile wallets, and cash on delivery where available. Exact options appear at checkout.
